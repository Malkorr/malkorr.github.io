The Definitive Guide to Becoming Cat Parents!
Play Hack

Find Toys That Your Cat Associates With You

Stick toys are a great option because you have to physically hold them to play with your kitty! You want your cat to see you playing with them so they associate you with the activity and happiness!
Sleep Hack

Slowly Blink to Show You're Relaxed

Slowly and softly blinking your eyes shows your cat you're relaxed. This relaxes your cat who will mimic the behaviour and calm down before bedtime.

Play Hack

Keep Toys Exciting

After a play sesh, put the toys out of sight to retain their novelty and excitement!

Environment Hack

Don't Poop Where You Eat

Keep your kitty's litter box in a quiet place away from their food or water source. If you have more than one cat, they will need at least one litter box each.

Food Hack

Create a Scavenger Hunt

Make your cat think, jump and explore by hiding treats in hard (but not impossible) to reach places around your apartment.

Play Hack

Teach Your Kitty Tricks

Teaching your cat tricks is a great way to bond with them. It's also a great way to get them mentally stimulated!
Environment Hack

Be Careful of Toxic Plants

Cats love to chew on plants but a lot of them can be toxic to their little bellies.

Environment Hack

Cat grass!

Cat grass is full of nutrients that your cat may not be getting elsewhere. It helps freshen their breath as well as aids in digestion and hairballs

Environment Hack

Install Cat Shelves or Stairs

Cats live in a vertical world. Having multi-level shelves on your wall or around the apartment is a great way to create resting places and a fun climbing space to keep them active.

Sleep Hack

Establish a Bedtime Routine

Shaneye and I don't let Cloud sleep in our bed at night. Cats tend to be very active in the early mornings and your sleep is important too!

Sleep Hack

Separation Anxiety

If you guys are finding that your kitty meows constantly when you leave the apartment or go to bed, try not to draw attention to the fact that you're leaving. Early on, Cloud would meow throughout the night when we went to bed so we stopped saying goodnight and just went to the bedroom.

Food Hack

Jackpot Treats

Find that treat your kitty can't resist! This will make them more responsive to training. We've found that freeze dried treats work best!
Food Hack

Nutrition is Very Important

Making sure your kitty is getting food that is both nutitious and delicious is the best way to ensure a healthy and happy kitty!
Play Hack

Play With Cat!

Playing with your cat is the best way to create a strong bond with them! This may seem obvious but can be difficult after a long and tiring day. If you feel exhausted, just remember, they've been waiting all day to spend some time with you!

Food Hack

Food & Water Bowls

Cats can get whisker fatigue if their food or water bowl is too smol. Make it easier on them and get a bowl thats big enough that their whiskers aren't constantly squished by the sides of the bowl.

Environment Hack

Make Sure the Litter Box is a Good Fit

Cats feel the most vulnerable when going to the bathroom! Some cats prefer litter boxes that are covered on all sides. Make sure it's big enough they can poop in private!

Environment Hack

On the Topic of Litter Boxes

Don't get an automatic litter box! It may seem tempting at first but they end up being a pain to clean and can break easily. Plus having to scoop pee & pewp is a great way to monitor your kitty's health!
Sleep Hack

Get a Cat Bed

Cloud refuses to use the bed and blanket we got him but he LOVES his window bed! It's a great way for him to chill and watch the world go by.
Food Hack

Debunk the Milk Myth

Drinking milk can lead to diarrhoea and obsesity in cats. Save it for your cereal.

Environment Hack

Leave Music On When You're Out

Leaving music on when you leave the apartment has been shown to have a calming effect on cats. Cloud likes to listen to Studio Ghibli songs.

Environment Hack

Provide a Kitty Hideaway

Every cat needs a hiding spot where they can go to feel safe (Cloud likes the pantry). Having a place they feel safe, like a cat tree, can do wonders for their overall happiness!
